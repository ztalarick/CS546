About Zachary Talarick

Hockey Career

I was born and raised in Burlington, New Jersey. I grew up an avid sports player; playing ice hockey for most of my life. I started hockey playing goalie for the Princeton Tigers, and I continued my goaltending until I played for the Mount Laurel Jaguars. The Jaguars are a competitive travel team; participating in tryouts are required to make the team. I continued my career with the Jaguars throughout highschool. After getting to college I began to play for the Stevens Club Ice Hockey team, the Ducks. I played for them for one year and stopped playing following an injury.
